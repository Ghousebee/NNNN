When to Bring Bread to a Table:: Bread is as important a part of hospitality at dinner as a glass of water. Some restaurants serve bread right away; others wait to serve bread until after the dinner order is taken. (This is to encourage hungry customers to order food.) If you choose to the do the latter in your restaurant, train servers to bring bread early if it is requested. Otherwise, they can bring bread after taking the dinner order.
